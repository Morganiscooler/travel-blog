🇮🇹 The Lake District 🇮🇹

My first stop in Italy was the Italian Lake District.

During this time, we went to an area right on the outskirts of Switzerland known as Como. It is a special town known for its centuries of silk production.

After arriving at lake Como, I decided to draw a quick sketch of the view over the lake. I had to sketch quickly because it was super hot and I didn't want to be left behind without the crew 🥲

Afterwards, I went exploring the town of Como with Mr. Guyer. There wasn't anything too special about the rather small town surrounding the lake, it was mostly just clothing stores and restaurants everywhere.

Then we went to go grab lunch, but I wasn't really hungry so I just bought a gelato (a special Italian ice cream)

Mr. Guyer saved a little bit of creamy fish rice on his plate for me and I got to try it (it was creamy and nice in my opinion). Apparently the fish used in the local Como restaurants all come from lake Como! 🐟

Before catching up with the rest of the crew, we explored the local cathedral in the city. Here are some photos I took of it:

After exploring the cathedral, I bought a keychain souvenir of Lake Como and met up with the rest of the crew. We then began heading out to our hotel we were going to stay for the next 2 nights.

The hotel was located in a very crappy area of Cinque Terre. There was a lot of trash surrounding it, along with suspicious people and weird graffitis on the walls. Here is what I ate for dinner next to the hotel:

I forgot to take a picture of the dessert I had, but it is called "Dolce del giorno"

Overall, I would rate the meal a 7/10. Personally I find authentic Italian food a little hard to finish because of how creamy it is, French food is definitely more suited for me.

Something cool about the restaurant next to the hotel we ate at is the fact it was right inbetween some local residents' housing complexes, so we were able to hear television music coming from their windows!

Before the tour group headed back to the hotel, me and Mr. Guyer decided to take a photo together in the ally way 📸🔥

Goodnight, and see you on my last (first) travel of the 2 weeks on the final page! 😉
